建议信

给图书馆管理员写信，提高图书馆服务

Dear Sir or Madam

I am a student of our universty who regularly comes to our library to do some reading. I’am writing this letter for the purpose of offering you some practical suggestings.

To begin with, the number of seats in our library is very limited and some students even save seats for others, will you please add us more seats? Furthermore, the water fountain on the second floor is unfunction, we will appreciate it if you can contact our staff member to repaire it for us.

Thank you for your time and consideration, I hope that you can enjoy you work and life.

Yours sincerely,

Li Ming.

投诉信

线上商店买的电子词典坏了， 投诉一波

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am one of the customers of you online store, I am writing this letter for the purpose of making a complaint about an e-dictionary which i brought from there three days ago.

To begin with, it is hard to boot up this device since the power button sometimes do not work. Furthermore, the battery can only last for three after it was fully charged. I will very appreciate it if you can change me a new one and I would like have this matter setted by the end of this weekend.

Thank you for your time and consideration, I am looking forward to your early reply.

Yours Sincerely,

Li Ming.

道歉信

从国外回来的时候， 把别人的音乐CD拿回来了， 道歉

Dear Bob,

I am writing this letter for the prupose of making a sincere apology to you for that I forgot to return the music CD which I borrowed from you last week. Yesterday when I landed in Beijing and found it was in my backpack. I am sorry that you can not listen to it at present.

To begin with, would you mind my sending it back to you via international express? However you have to wait at least ten days. Furthermore, it is possible for you to buy another one in your local store and I would pay for it. If you have any other solutions please feel free and let me know.

Once again, I am sorry for any inconvenience caused. I am looking forward to your early reply.

Yours Sincerely,

Li Ming.

辞职信

现在的工作跟自己的专业不符，辞职

Dear Mr.Wang

I am a staff member of your company, I am writing this letter for the purpose of resigning from my current position. I was employed as an editor two months ago, thank you very much for your trust.

To begin with, since I was majored in computer science in college I found that my skills does not match my current job and I want working with a job that I am interested in. Furthermore, I decide to continue my education thus I plan to take part in the exams at the end of this year. Therefor all my time and energy should be devote to the prepare of the exam.

I am sorry for any inconvenience caused. I hope you can accept my apology and understand my situation.

Yours Sincerely,
Li Ming.
